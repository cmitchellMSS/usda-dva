Goal

With over 43 Million citizens using SNAP, we're always looking for opportunities keep users at the center of technology innovation. Our prototype streams USDA data from Data.gov, and other open source data sets to collate farmers market location, retailers, and key SNAP offices to ensure citizens have access to the right information at the right time. Stay tuned for more features (i.e. transit data, search for your favorite retailers, and localized ratings).
